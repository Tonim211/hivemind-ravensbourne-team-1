• Fragmented Communication & Info: New starters struggle with a lack of a
structured communication portal, making it difficult to adjust to company culture.
Information is scattered across Teams, SharePoint, WhatsApp, Email, and physical
notice boards.
• The "First Week" Fog: Participants reported feeling "lost" or "overwhelmed" due to
a lack of clear daily priorities or a list of pending tasks.
• Workspace Setup Hurdles: There is often no clear assistance or guide for setting up
a new hire's physical or digital workspace during their first days.
• Version Control & Accuracy Risks: Users waste time "double-checking"
information with managers because they cannot tell which digital document is the
latest version. High-stakes environments (like engineering) face increased stress due
to the fear of making errors with outdated data.
• Generic "Box-Ticking" Onboarding: Early training feels like a generic routine
rather than role-specific guidance that provides practical day-to-day skills.
• AI Trust & Verification Gap: Users find AI useful but frustrating if the output
depends on a "perfect prompt". Most will not trust AI answers unless they see direct
links to company sources or have a "human judge" verify the facts.
